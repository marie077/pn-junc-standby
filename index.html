P-N Junction + Solar Cell

In a solar cell, the P-N junction plays a crucial role in converting sunlight into electricity. When photons hit the junction, they excite electrons, creating electron-hole pairs. The built-in electric field at the junction separates these charges, directing electrons toward the n-side and holes toward the p-side, generating a current that can be harnessed for power. This photovoltaic effect is the key mechanism behind solar energy harvesting.
